CASH THAT TIME

MOTION GRAPHICS | ILLUSTRATION | 2019
For my final year project at university, I created a motion graphics video called ‘Cash That Time.’ The piece explores different themes on how we perceive money and time. The driving force behind the project was to create something quirky and unique yet meaningful. The aim was to encourage audiences to think more holistically, helping change perspectives on how our time should be valued. For this project, I designed and animated using applications Adobe Illustrator and Adobe After Effects.

COINVESTOR LIMITED

BRANDING | WEB DESIGN | | UX DESIGN | 2018
During my placement year at CoInvestor Limited, the company underwent a rebrand creating two new brands, CoInvestor Technologies, and CoInvestor Connect. JINX MAGAZINE

GRAPHIC DESIGN | BRANDING | ILLUSTRATION | 2015
Jinx is a slick, illustrative magazine aimed at teenagers 16- 20. The magazine explores different subject manners such as music, film, education, festivals and also politics. I created Jinx as part of my A2 graphics coursework, where I was responsible for creating a logo, front cover, magazine articles and poster for my brand. I composed and illustrated these designs using both Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

SAVE THE SEA

GAME DESIGN | ILLUSTRATION | 2019
Save The Sea is an educational mobile game targeted at 6 - 11 year olds. The aim of the game is to collect as much rubbish from the sea whilst avoiding hitting the fish. I designed all gaming assets including the menu pages, plastic icons, fishes and the different gaming worlds. It was learned that kids are stimulated by bright colours and imaginative characters. Therefore to ensure the target audience was attracted a vibrant blue house style was inherited to reflect the sea and memorable characters were created including the scuba diver.

OCCUPY WALL STREET

GRAPHIC DESIGN | BRANDING | ILLUSTRATION | 2015
Occupy Wall Street is a people-powered movement that began on September 17, 2011, in Manhattan’s Financial District. OWS is the fight back against the corrosive power of major banks and the role of Wall Street.

As part of my A2 Graphics examinations I rebranded the movement creating a logo, leaflet, and poster to help raise awareness and as a catalyst to reignite OWS. I kept a very minimal black and white house style using bright colours to add impact to the design. The leaflet is composed of interesting typographic elements as well as illustrations making it aesthetically pleasing for the viewer to read and take interest in the political subject.
